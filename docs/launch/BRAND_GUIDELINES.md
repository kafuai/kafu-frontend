## Brand Personality
- Executive
- Professional
- Intelligent
- Trusted
- Modern
- Calm

## Design Principles
- Executive First
- Simplicity
- Clarity
- Consistency
- Enterprise Grade

Professional, confident, concise, business-focused.

Every product, presentation, website, document, demo and AI-generated asset must follow the KAFU AI brand identity.
