# Business Challenges

## 1. Fragmented Executive Visibility
Leadership depends on separate reports from Finance, HR, Operations, and Strategy.

## 2. Payroll Readiness Pressure
HR spends significant time at month-end validating attendance, absence, overtime, leaves, and payroll exceptions before sending payroll inputs to Finance.

## 3. Delayed Strategic Initiatives
Several transformation initiatives are delayed due to weak cross-functional visibility.

## 4. Workforce Risk
Certain departments show higher absence and turnover risk.

## 5. Operational Exceptions
Operations has recurring issues around delayed tasks, compliance gaps, and unresolved risks.

## 6. Decision Latency
Executives receive information after issues already become urgent.
