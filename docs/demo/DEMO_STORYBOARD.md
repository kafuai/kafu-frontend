# KAFU AI Executive Demo Storyboard

## Demo Goal
Make the executive understand KAFU AI value within the first 10 minutes.

## Audience
- CEO
- COO
- CFO
- CHRO
- Executive Committee

## Story
A growing enterprise has fragmented visibility across finance, operations, HR, and execution. KAFU AI gives leadership one intelligent operating layer to understand what is happening, why it matters, and what to do next.

## Opening Moment
Start with the Executive Dashboard and show company health, alerts, KPIs, and AI recommendations immediately.

## Wow Moment
KAFU AI connects multiple business signals and generates an executive briefing that turns scattered data into clear decisions.

## Closing Moment
End by showing that KAFU AI can become the executive operating system for the organization.
